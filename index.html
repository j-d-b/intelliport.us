About Us

IntelliPort, Inc. is a software development company that uses technology and analytics to boost productivity by optimizing system efficiency. We focus on intelligent optimization in the container terminal industry.

Background

Our company and venture results from people with extensive experience from various operations around the world as well as an understanding of systems and software.

Our products result from operational needs; current terminal yard operations leave ample room for innovation, especially in terms of yield improvement—that is, maximizing the profitability of existing investments in terminal infrastructure.

We have tested and validated our products with our associated companies. Furthermore, our optimization algorithms are backed by academic research performed at top universities.

Smart Modules

IntelliPort offers commercial smart modules that utilize advance decision support technology.

These modules integrate with a Terminal Operating System and/or a Enterprise Resource Planning system to create a comprehensive port management solution. Interfaces can be built between the different systems or this integration can be done manually.

Our modules are designed with a flexible architecture that allows them to be licensed in the traditional manner or utilized as a service through a cloud-based platform.

Products

In pursuit of creating novel products, IntelliPort has directed research and development towards three products:

Dynamic Schedule Module (DSM)

DSM

Optimized human resource allocation based on demand

The Dynamic Schedule Module automates and optimizes staff scheduling by minimizing cost while ensuring equity to all parties.

The DSM offers two tools:

A simulator helps recruitment planners determine hiring needs by generating the number of required employees over a selected time interval (e.g. weeks, months, years).

A schedule generator builds cost-efficient schedules based on forecasted demand. It remains flexible by allowing schedules to be updated as demand changes by employing a concept of 'on-call' to adapt to short-notice changes in ship schedules.

The DSM's unique design rotates employees across equipment and time-slots to ensure equal opportunity for success, which allows for productivity-based incentives.

Visit the DSM project page for additional information.

Relocation Optimization Module (ROM)

ROM

Minimizing unproductive container moves

The Relocation Optimization Module uses expected container retrieval order to maximize yard efficiency by minimizing the number of future relocations.

The ROM has two functions:

  1. Determining where to locate a blocking container within a block during the retrieval of a requested container
  2. Determining where to stack a new container within a block

At a high level, the module takes in the container ID of the container to be retrieved (1) or the container to be stacked (2) and outputs the optimized set of moves that should be taken to complete this action.

Predicted Retrieval Time Generator (PRTG)

PRTG

Predicting container retrieval time/date

The Predicted Retrieval Time Generator forecasts the arrivals of freight forwards and truckers servicing container terminals. The PRTG considers available data to identify patterns in the arrival/departure of containers and utilizes these patterns to predict when any given import container will be retrieved.

The PRTG generates a pick-up probability for each import container for future time windows. This information offers valuable insight that allows containers to be stacked in positions that will minimize future relocations.
